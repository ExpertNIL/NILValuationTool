Valuation Engine

Not just “market value” — context value. Grades and scores are generated from fair-market bands, position multipliers, eligibility curves, and budget share logic.

How scoring works (high level)

  • Fair-market bands anchor expected ranges by tier, position, and eligibility.
  • Position multipliers adjust value for roles (e.g., QB premium).
  • Budget-aware grading penalizes over-allocation as % of annual budget.
  • Outputs: internal score (0–1000) → operator-friendly letter grades.

Operator tools built on top

  • Target Player Valuation: input production/potential/physicals → budget-aware NIL range.
  • Roster composition: identify spend concentration by unit and tier.
  • Alerts: surface budget risk, gaps, and “must act” flags.
  • Export-ready reporting for leadership conversations.
